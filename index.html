I am a Data Analyst for Enterprise-Scale CRM, Lifecycle & Marketing Analytics. My career has been defined by one mission: bridging the gap between "Tech" and "Business."

Whether it's diagnosing a funnel leak for AutoZone or optimizing logistics routes for Sonic GMBH, I focus on the metric that matters most: Impact.
